# AM35 Logo Exploration

> Summary of logo design exploration session, April 7, 2026.

## Concept Direction

The strongest direction is **"Angular Lines"** — a mark built entirely from straight lines at consistent angles:

- **A** = two lines meeting at a peak: /\
- **M** = three downstrokes, where **A's right leg doubles as M's first stroke** — so 4 visible lines total
- **35** = Inter weight 900 in blue gradient below the mark

### Three M Variations Explored

1. **Parallel offset** — Three \'s equally spaced, all same angle. Structured, clean.
2. **Fan from peak** — All three M strokes radiate from A's peak. Dynamic, speed-line energy.
3. **Spread from peak** — Same angle but staggered start points cascading from the peak area.

**No winner picked yet** — session ended before a final direction was chosen. Resume here.

### What Was Rejected
- Angular/geometric 3 and 5 numerals (too abstract, illegible, "pretty bad")
- Diamond lattice (AM peaks mirrored as 35 valleys — too abstract to read as numbers)
- Chevron-style 3 and > shapes
- Seven-segment style numerals
- All attempts to make 35 from the same line construction as AM

### What Works
- The A as /\ (two lines, peak) — confirmed correct
- M as three downstrokes, not a zigzag /\/
- A's left line overlapping M's first line (shared stroke)
- 35 in Inter 900 — just use the font, don't try to construct the numbers from lines
- White AM + blue gradient 35 color treatment
- The mark works at icon scale (favicon) through hero scale
