TOP PLAY #12 • vs THUNDER

🎬 Highlights

Design flex: Scroll-driven video playback. As you scroll, clips scrub through. Hover thumbnails to preview. Featured play takes the full viewport with cinematic letterboxing. Filter by game, play type. Each clip feels like a SportsCenter Top 10 moment.

📸 Gallery

Design flex: Masonry grid with cursor-reactive tilt (3D perspective shift on hover). Images scale up smoothly into a full-screen lightbox. Infinite scroll loading. Filter by game/practice/lifestyle. Every photo has that parallax depth — the gallery itself feels 3D.
