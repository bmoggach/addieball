What kind of interactions get you hyped?

Pick everything that excites you — this is your design playground 🏀

A

🎬 Scroll-Driven Cinematics

The page IS the experience. Video plays as you scroll, elements fly in with parallax, text reveals frame-by-frame. Think Apple product pages meets Nike athlete stories. The scroll bar is the director.

B

🌐 3D / WebGL World

A basketball floating in 3D space that you can spin. Particle effects trailing behind cursor movement. Maybe a mini basketball court you navigate through. Three.js / Spline vibes — the site feels like a game.

C

✨ Butter-Smooth Micro-Interactions

Every hover, click, and transition is buttery and intentional. Magnetic buttons, elastic scrolling, text that splits apart on hover, images that tilt with your cursor. Death by a thousand beautiful details.

D

📸 Media-First Showcase

Massive full-bleed photos and video that dominate the viewport. Lightbox galleries, before/after sliders, auto-playing highlight reels, Ken Burns zoom effects. The media IS the design — everything else gets out of the way.

E

🔤 Giant Typography & Motion Text

Words that take up the entire screen. Text that animates letter-by-letter, strokes that draw themselves, marquees that scroll infinitely. The typography IS the art — think award-winning agency sites.

F

🖱️ Custom Cursor & Mouse-Driven FX

Custom cursor that morphs over elements, mouse trail effects, elements that react to your pointer position. Hover states that are mini-experiences. The cursor becomes part of the design language.

Select as many as you want — we're building a playground, not picking one trick
